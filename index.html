Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Dan Brown adventures in Prague.

Our experiences are inspired by the themes, locations, and puzzle-solving elements found in Dan Brown's novels. We incorporate cryptographic puzzles, historical mysteries, and the atmospheric storytelling that makes his books so compelling, all set against Prague's stunning medieval backdrop.
Private tours typically last 2-3 hours, depending on your group's pace and interests. We can customize the duration to fit your schedule, with options for shorter 90-minute highlights tours or extended 4-hour deep-dive experiences for true mystery enthusiasts.
Private tours accommodate up to 8 people for the best experience. For larger groups, we can arrange multiple guides or split into smaller teams. The escape room experience works best with 2-6 participants to ensure everyone can actively participate in solving the puzzles.
Prague's covered passages, churches, and historic buildings provide plenty of shelter during light rain. For heavy weather, we can reschedule at no charge or adapt the tour to focus on indoor locations. The escape room experience is completely indoors and unaffected by weather.
