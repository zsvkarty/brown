Tour Itinerary

A carefully crafted journey through Prague's most mysterious locations, each with its own secrets to uncover.

1

Old Town Square - The Heart of Mystery

Begin your journey at Prague's most famous square, where we'll decode the astronomical clock's hidden symbols and explore the connection between medieval astronomy and Dan Brown's themes of science versus religion.

Duration: 30 minutes
2

Týn Church - Gothic Secrets

Explore the mysterious Týn Church with its twin spires that hide alchemical symbols. Learn about Prague's role as a center of medieval learning and the secret societies that once gathered here.

Duration: 25 minutes
3

Jewish Quarter - Codes and Kabbalah

Discover the cryptographic traditions of Prague's Jewish community, including the legend of the Golem and the mystical practices that influenced European esotericism for centuries.

Duration: 40 minutes
4

Prague Castle - Imperial Mysteries

Ascend to Prague Castle to uncover the secrets of Rudolf II's court, where alchemists, astronomers, and mystics gathered. Explore St. Vitus Cathedral's hidden symbolism and the castle's role in European occult history.

Duration: 45 minutes
5

Lesser Town - Hidden Passages

Navigate through secret passages and hidden courtyards in Lesser Town, where baroque palaces conceal centuries-old mysteries. Solve your final cryptographic puzzle in a location known only to locals.

Duration: 30 minutes
