about us
Walk through Prague, exploring the locations where Dan Brown&#39;s novel The Secret of
Secrets takes place. Your guide will be a Prague native who will take you to places the
average tourist never gets to see. You will see the famous Astronomical Clock (Orloj), the
Church of Our Lady before Týn, the Týn Literary Café, and Black Angel&#39;s Bar on the Old
Town Square; in the adjacent Jewish Quarter, you will view the Old Jewish Cemetery and the
Old-New Synagogue. You will pass through the Klementinum, home to a grand library and
the Astronomical Tower with its observatory. You will have a coffee at the Four Seasons
Hotel, cross the Charles Bridge to the Lesser Town, and walk past the American Embassy up
to the Petřín Lookout Tower and the mirror maze. You will conclude the tour at Folimanka
Park and the Bastion u Božích muk.
